The Technology Behind Talk To The Hand

Our sign language detection system utilizes cutting-edge machine learning algorithms trained on a vast dataset of sign language gestures. This enables real-time recognition of sign language, empowering users to learn and communicate effectively.

We are committed to open-source development, and our platform leverages several open-source libraries for sign language recognition, speech synthesis, and user interface development. We believe in sharing our knowledge and contributing to the accessibility community.
